Architecture & Core Contracts

Primary components and responsibilities (short):

  • ARCGovernor: Proposal lifecycle management, multiple voting modes.
  • ARCTimelock: Secure execution, proposers/executors, emergency controls.
  • ARCTreasury / ARCDAO: Fund orchestration and coordinated governance actions.
  • NFT Subsystem: EvolvingCompanion (ERC721), TraitVault (ERC1155), TokenBoundAccount modules.

Design emphasis: modularity, upgradeability (UUPS), and role-based access control for secure evolution.

Security, Ops & Risk Controls

  • Multisig safes for treasury and ecosystem funds.
  • Role-based AccessControl for upgrades and emergency operations.
  • Timelock enforced execution for governance decisions.
  • Audit posture: standardized patterns (OpenZeppelin upgradeable + UUPS) — recommend periodic audits for new modules.

Key Risks (concise)

  • Privileged admin keys — mitigated by multisig + timelock but requires ongoing rotation / governance policies.
  • Smart contract bugs in new NFT modules — recommend targeted audits & staged deployments.

Stakeholder Incentives & Engagement

Governance Participation

Voting weight and early proposer privileges aligned with token stewardship. Incentivize active delegates via targeted rewards approved by on-chain proposals.

Developer Bounties & Audits

Structured bounties for module contributions and a prioritized audit fund to secure new releases. Public bug disclosure and reward process recommended.

NFT Ecosystem Engagement

XP progression and trait unlock mechanisms create on-chain incentives for long-term holders and community contributors.

Liquidity Stewardship

Transparent LP policy and periodic on-chain reports to align market makers, stakers, and treasury managers.
